Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

ReelTime is a personal mobile application developed for entertainment purposes. Your privacy is important, and this Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Information Collection and Use

ReelTime does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. The app does not require user registration or account creation.

The app accesses publicly available third-party movie APIs to display movie information such as titles, descriptions, and images. This data is fetched in real time and is not stored on any backend servers.

Data Storage

Any temporary data (such as cached images) is stored locally on your device to improve performance. This data can be removed at any time by clearing the app’s storage or uninstalling the app.

Third-Party Services

ReelTime may use third-party services to retrieve movie-related content. These services operate under their own privacy policies, and ReelTime does not control how they collect or use data.

Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect information processed by the app. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

ReelTime does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
